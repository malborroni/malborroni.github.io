About Me

After graduating in Economics from the University of Milano-Bicocca, I decided to start a new educational path in Data Science, at the same University.

For over two years, I have been improving my programming skills, trying to provide solutions to tasks of the most varied types; all always aiming at the union of technical skills and creativity in the proposed solution.
Lost in love with Data Visualization in all its forms.

I have always had a strong passion for mathematics and statistics, which has attracted a great interest in the world of finance, born thanks to the courses attended during the last year of the Bachelor's Degree.
Thanks to the Master's Degree Program I am developing a great interest in areas such as Machine Learning and Deep Learning, with a focus on everything related to the world of image processing.

The years of higher education have given me the opportunity to discover, through extra-curricular courses, the world of photography, making me a great fan of it.
